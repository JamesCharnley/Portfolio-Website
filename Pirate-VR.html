Pirate VR

Description

A prototype for a vitual reality wave based combat game. Be a pirate that must protect their ship from sea monsters.

Development

This video game was collaboratively developed by a team of programmers and artists as part of a university project using Unity and C#. The project was focused on making a vertical slice of a virtual reality game.
